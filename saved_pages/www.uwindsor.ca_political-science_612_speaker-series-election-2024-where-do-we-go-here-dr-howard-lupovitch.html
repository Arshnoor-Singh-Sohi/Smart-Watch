Speaker Series: Election 2024: Where do we go from here? with Dr. Howard Lupovitch

Wednesday, November 13, 2024 - 12:00

On Wednesday, November 13, 2024 at 12:00 pm, Dr. Howard Lupovitch of Wayne State University  will deliver a talk titled "Election 2024: Where do we go from here?" as part of the University of Windsor Department of Political Science Speaker Series.

Dr. Howard Lupovitch is a professor of history and director of the Cohn-Haddow Center for Judaic Studies at Wayne State.
