How to Set Time on G-SHOCK Watches - Daylight Savings Time (DST)

Daylight Savings Time (DST) begins on the second Sunday in March and ends on the first Sunday of November every year. To adjust the time on your Casio G-SHOCK watch, you will need to look up the product manual for your specific watch module. The instructions will vary for each watch model, because each watch has a different module powering the functionality. Follow these instructions to find the support module on how to change the time for your particular G-SHOCK watch:
