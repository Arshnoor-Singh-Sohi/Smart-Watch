When a connected power meter is detected, Ultra 2 automatically estimates your Functional Threshold Power (FTP), the highest level of intensity you can theoretically maintain for an hour. Your FTP enables Power Zones, which show what zone you’re in and track how long you spend in each one to improve your performance. Swimming A stroke of genius. Cellular waypoints are automatically added to the Compass app, so you can retrace your steps to make a call or send a text, or even call emergency services.8 Diving You’re never out of your depth. Whether it’s scuba diving, free diving, snorkeling, or high-speed water sports, Apple Watch Ultra 2 is made for it all. Certified to EN13319 and capable of diving to 40 meters, it’s got all the core features you need to break new ground in the water. Dive into more details. A full dive computer at hand. Designed in partnership with Huish Outdoors, the Oceanic+ app turns Ultra 2 into a fully capable dive computer running a Bühlmann decompression algorithm.9 Simply turn the Digital Crown to access additional screens that make complex information easy to understand. And you’ll get all the safety warnings you’d expect, while the app constantly calculates and monitors your dive parameters. Get the Oceanic+ app Location is everything. Plan your dive using Location Planning and the Oceanic+ app on iPhone. Heat maps help you find popular spots, while providing local conditions with community feedback about the area. Come up for air and info. Ultra 2 syncs automatically to the Oceanic+ app on your iPhone and provides data from your dive quickly after surfacing, including a summary of your dive profile, and critical dive parameters. It also makes it easy to search through past dives and pictures and share your experiences with friends and family. Go with the flow. The Tides app shows seven days of information including rising, falling, high, and low tides, sunrise, and sunset. And you can check conditions at shorelines around the world, or search over 115,000 beaches by name. Dark. Art. The parachute-style buckle has dual side-release buttons that close with an audible click. While rugged enough for scuba, it’s also elegant enough to wear while out at night. The Vitals app establishes a typical range for each of your overnight health metrics, like your heart rate, respiratory rate, wrist temperature, and sleep duration.10 A quick glance lets you track changes over time. Eye-opening data. The Sleep app doesn’t just keep track of your time asleep. It also shows how much time you spent in REM, Core, or Deep sleep, and helps create a schedule and a bedtime routine so you can meet your sleep goals. Sleep apnea notifications. Sleep apnea is a condition where breathing momentarily stops during sleep. If untreated, it can potentially lead to hypertension, type 2 diabetes, and cardiac issues. Ultra 2 can now monitor breathing disturbances and notify you of possible sleep apnea. Making it easier to get the help you need for better rest and recovery.11 Keeps an eye on your heart. Apple Watch Ultra 2 has a water resistance rating of 100 meters under ISO standard 22810:2010. It may be used for recreational scuba diving (with compatible third-party app from the App Store) to 40 meters and high-speed water sports. Apple Watch Ultra 2 should not be used for diving below 40 meters. Water resistance is not a permanent condition and can diminish over time. For additional information see support.apple.com/HT205000. Apple Watch Ultra 2 is rated IP6X dust resistant. Battery and performance may be affected at very low temperatures.
