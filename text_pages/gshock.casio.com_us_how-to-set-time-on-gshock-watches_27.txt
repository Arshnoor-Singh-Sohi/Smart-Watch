To adjust the time on your Casio G-SHOCK watch, you will need to look up the product manual for your specific watch module. The instructions will vary for each watch model, because each watch has a different module powering the functionality. Follow these instructions to find the support module on how to change the time for your particular G-SHOCK watch: How Do I Set or Adjust the Time on my Casio G-SHOCK watch?
